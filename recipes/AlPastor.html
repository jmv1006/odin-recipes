Tacos Al Pastor

Description

This recipe is for authentic Tacos De Al Pastor. One of my favorites, I find the marinade to be very flavorful, with hints of cumin, pineapple, and cinammon. You will surely enjoy these tacos.

Ingredients

  • Pork Shoulder
  • Pineapple
  • Onion and Cilantro
  • Pineapple Juice
  • Kosher Salt
  • Chiles Guajillos
  • Chiles Anchos
    •

    Steps

    1. Marinate the meat
    2. Add diced pineapple to the meat
    3. Grill meat until it is dark red in color and easy to cut
    4. Feel free to make tacois, burritos, or anything else to your liking with the meat!
